Hide All Apps: The Smart Way to Keep Your Phone Private

Let's be honest—your phone probably has a few apps you'd rather keep to yourself. Banking apps, dating profiles, private messages, or maybe just that game you don't want your coworkers knowing about.

The easiest fix? Hide all apps you want to keep private, without uninstalling anything.

Why Hide Apps?

Most people hide apps for one of three reasons:

  • Privacy – Not everyone needs to see your financial apps or personal chats
  • Less clutter – Fewer icons mean a cleaner, less distracting home screen
  • Peace of mind – When you hand your phone to someone, you don't have to worry about what they might stumble into

How Hide All Works

Hide All is an Android app that lets you hide all apps you choose inside a secure, password-protected space. But here's the clever part—it doesn't look like a security app.

The icon is a calculator. Open it, and it works like a regular calculator. Add some numbers, get a result. Nothing suspicious.

But enter your secret PIN on that calculator keypad, and suddenly it unlocks a hidden vault. Inside, all the apps, photos, and videos you've tucked away are right there, ready to use.

To anyone else, it's just a calculator.

What Makes It Different

Most app hiders just remove icons from your home screen. The apps are still visible in your app drawer or recent tasks. Hide All takes a different approach.

When you hide all apps through Hide All, they're stored in a protected environment—not just hidden from view. They won't show up in recent tasks, app searches, or system settings. They're genuinely tucked away.

Key Features

  • Calculator disguise – Looks and works like a real calculator. No one would guess.
  • PIN or pattern lock – Only you can access the hidden space.
  • Hide apps, photos, and videos – Not just apps—any file you want to keep private.
  • No root required – Works on standard Android devices. No warranty issues.
  • Lightweight – Runs without slowing down your phone.
  • No ads – Just a clean, focused privacy tool.
  • Supports 50+ apps – Hide as many as you need.

How to Use It

Setting it up takes about a minute:

  1. Download Hide All from the Google Play Store
  2. Open the app—it looks like a calculator
  3. Set up a PIN or pattern
  4. Tap to select which apps to hide
  5. That's it. They're gone from your home screen and app drawer

To unhide, just long-press the icon and exit hidden mode.

Who Is It For?

  • Anyone who shares their phone – Family, friends, coworkers
  • People who value privacy – Financial apps, private messages, personal photos
  • Travelers – Extra protection when crossing borders
  • Parents – Keep certain apps away from kids

You don't need to be paranoid to want a little privacy on your phone. Hide All gives you a simple, discreet way to hide all apps you'd rather keep to yourself—without deleting anything, without rooting your phone, and without anyone knowing it's there.

It's just a calculator. Unless you know the code.
