How to Hide Apps on the iPhones

To hide apps on iPhones, methods vary by iOS version and desired privacy level. Below is a comprehensive guide covering all official techniques and limitations, based on iOS features up to the latest iOS 18:

üîí 1. iOS 18: Native "Hidden Apps" Feature (Most Secure)

Best for: Complete invisibility (main screen, search, widgets, notifications).

Steps:

  1. Long-press the app icon ‚Üí Select "Require Face ID" ‚Üí Tap "Hide and Require Face ID"
  2. Authenticate with Face ID or passcode.
  3. The app vanishes from all interfaces (including Settings and Spotlight).

Access hidden apps:

  • Swipe to the App Library (rightmost home screen).
  • Tap the "Hidden" folder ‚Üí Authenticate ‚Üí Open the app

Restore hidden apps:

  • Unlock the "Hidden" folder ‚Üí Long-press the app ‚Üí "Add to Home Screen".

Limitations:

  • Built-in apps (e.g., Safari, Mail) cannot be hidden;
  • Hidden apps cannot use widgets.

2. Hide from Spotlight Search:

  • Go to Settings ‚Üí Siri & Search ‚Üí Select app ‚Üí Turn off "Show in Search"

Limitations:

  • Apps remain visible in the App Library.
  • Notifications still appear.
