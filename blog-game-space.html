What Is a Game Space?

A game space is a feature—either built into your device or provided by third‑party apps—that creates a separate, optimized area for your games. It typically offers:

  • Game‑focused performance enhancements (clearing RAM, blocking notifications)
  • Privacy controls (hiding games from prying eyes)
  • Multi‑account management (running multiple game profiles simultaneously)
